Why YOOBEE?

Yoobee School of Design is New Zealand's largest private tertiary provider of creative digital technology courses and related resources.

We have an international reputation for producing industry-fit creative professionals and our graduates have gone on to work in a huge variety of successful creative practices all over the globe.

Student Space

Students of all ages and backgrounds study with us. Some are school leavers, while others have taken time out from the workplace to retrain. We also cater for international students.

Reflecting real-life industry work environments, our campuses are situated in modern, multi-storied buildings in central city locations across New Zealand.
